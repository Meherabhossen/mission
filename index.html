History Of

Nanjing University

Nanjing University established in 1958, the history of Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications can be traced to the training class for the cadres of the General Postal Administrations during the wartime in 1942. Thereafter, it underwent a series of name and structural changes, including the Posts and Telecommunications Training Department of Shandong University and the Shandong Postal School. Zhao Zhigang, Chief of the Wartime General Postal Administration then, served concurrently as the head teacher of the training class and the principal of the school in succession during the period from 1942 to 1947.
